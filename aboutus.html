ABOUT US


Since 2015

Cookie Mix began as a small-scale cookie business in 2015. Its founder, Ms. Siti Aisyah, began her cookie business as a pastime, but as cookies became popular and orders increased, she decided to make the cookie business a full-time job.

Currently, there are two types of chocolate chip cookies that are freshly prepared throughout the day. By using the best ingredients, such as semi-sweet chocolate chips and delicious almonds, these homemade-tasting cookies have become famous merely by word of mouth.

Because of the use of high-quality ingredients and unequalled crunchiness and deliciousness, the cookie mix has improved significantly and is now known as the most delicious homemade cookie recipe in Malaysia.
